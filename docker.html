    • 4) Summery by Mark

    Imagine you make some hospital software that the nurses use to do something - clock in or whatever. You do that in react.
    Every week the hospital wants to make the system bigger/better somehow, and do more. So you're always coding and pushing your code out for the nurses to use.
    Before you push, you have to test the system completely to ensure it's not broken. You can use Docker or whatever else to automate the testing so you don't have to spend 3 days manually pushing every button. Seems like a PITA to setup, but better than doing the same thing over and over and over and over...
